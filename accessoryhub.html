Glasses & Eyewear

Eyewear is more than just a vision aid — it is a fashion statement. From bold frames to minimalist designs, the right pair of glasses can transform your entire look and boost your confidence.

Types of Glasses

Aviator
Classic teardrop-shaped lenses with thin metal frames, originally designed for pilots in the 1930s.
Wayfarer
Bold, angular frames popularised by Ray-Ban in the 1950s. Suits almost every face shape.
Round
Circular lenses that give a vintage, intellectual look. Great for square face shapes.
Cat-Eye
Upswept outer edges giving a retro feminine look. Best for round and heart-shaped faces.
Blue Light Blocking
Filters harmful blue light from screens, reducing eye strain during long hours of computer use.

How to Choose the Right Frame

  1. Determine your face shape: oval, round, square, heart, or oblong
  2. Choose a frame shape that contrasts your face shape for balance
  3. Consider your skin tone when selecting frame colour
  4. Try multiple styles and sizes before committing
  5. Ensure the nose bridge and temples fit comfortably

Watches & Timepieces

A watch is more than a time-telling device — it is a symbol of personality and craftsmanship. From sleek minimalist analogs to feature-packed smartwatches, there is a timepiece for every wrist and lifestyle.

Watch Movement Types

Quartz
Battery-powered and highly accurate. Low maintenance and affordable. The most common type.
Mechanical
Powered by a hand-wound mainspring. Appreciated for its craftsmanship and traditional value.
Automatic
Self-winding mechanical movement powered by the natural motion of the wearer's wrist.
Solar
Powered by light energy. Eco-friendly and very low maintenance.

How to Pick the Right Watch

  1. Decide the occasion: everyday casual, business formal, or sport
  2. Choose a movement type based on your lifestyle and budget
  3. Select a strap material: leather, stainless steel, or silicone
  4. Check water resistance rating if you are active or outdoors
  5. Ensure the case diameter matches your wrist size

Necklaces & Chains

Necklaces are one of the most versatile accessories in fashion. A well-chosen necklace can elevate a simple outfit, add a touch of elegance, or make a bold fashion statement.

Types of Necklaces

Chain Necklace
Simple linked chains in gold, silver, or platinum. A classic everyday staple for both men and women.
Pendant Necklace
Features a decorative element hanging from the chain, often personalised with initials or birthstones.
Choker
Sits close to the neck. Available in velvet, metal, lace, or beaded styles.
Layered Necklace
Multiple chains of different lengths worn together for a trendy, stacked look.
Statement Necklace
Bold, eye-catching pieces designed to be the centrepiece of any outfit.

Care & Maintenance Tips

  1. Store necklaces in separate pouches to prevent tangling
  2. Remove jewellery before swimming, showering, or applying perfume
  3. Clean gently with a soft lint-free cloth after each use
  4. Keep silver pieces in an airtight bag to slow tarnishing
  5. Take precious pieces to a professional jeweller for annual cleaning
